The Problem

Your best lead just called your competitor.

Most small firms lose 40–60% of potential clients not on the merits of the case — but because of how long it takes to respond. Here's where it breaks down.

Slow response loses the client

Studies show that leads contacted within 5 minutes are 21x more likely to convert. A missed call on a Tuesday afternoon usually means the case goes to the firm that answered first.

Inconsistent follow-up

Leads that don't answer the first time rarely get a second or third touch. Your pipeline quietly leaks revenue every week because no one has time to chase each prospect.

Intake is eating billable hours

Partners and paralegals burn hours every week on unqualified calls, scheduling, and follow-ups — time that should be spent on cases that actually generate revenue.
